Wedding

Ceremony and Reception

5:00 PM, June 23, 2018
Haus Sanssouci
Am Großen Wannsee 60, 14109 Berlin

Haus Sanssouci is a restaurant about thirty minutes south of the center of Berlin in the Zehlendorf district. It lies on the Großer Wannsee lake. The ceremony (freie Trauung) will take place in the garden beyond the restaurant's back terrace at 5:00 PM (17:00).

A cocktail hour will follow the ceremony. Dinner and dancing will take place on the restaurant's second floor, the entirety of which will be available to our guests for the night.

The dress code is semi-formal. Please note that the ceremony will be mobile phone free to ensure the best experience for everyone.
